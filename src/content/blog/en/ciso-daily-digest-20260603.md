## Dashlane Password Manager Users Locked Out in Widespread Brute-Force Attacks

Users of the Dashlane password manager have been locked out of their accounts due to widespread brute-force attacks targeting the service. The attacks triggered account lockout mechanisms, preventing legitimate users from accessing their password vaults. Dashlane has confirmed the incident and is working on restoring access for affected users while implementing additional rate-limiting and anomaly detection measures to prevent further abuse.
